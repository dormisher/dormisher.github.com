about me

about me

My name is Daniel Ormisher, and this is my site. I am a web developer, and on here you can find information about me and what I'm interested in. See what I do, and see examples of my work.

As a developer I consider myself 'tech agnostic', i.e. I'm open to any programming language / framework that may spark my interest. I don't display fanatic zeal at the anticipation of a new release of .NET, and you won't see me glare at a Ruby developer with disdain, but if there's something interesting in a tool that might make my life a bit easier - then that's cool. I just really enjoy making cool stuff for the web, and working with anything that helps me to do that.

If you want to speak to me for any reason please get in touch via email or twitter or whatever.
